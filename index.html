About Chennai Students Kondattam

CSK 6.0
Fellow Driven Fest

Chennai Students Kondattam is a place faciliatated by fellows for students to realise their potential beyond classroom space.

A two day annual event for ALL the children of Teach for India Chennai. It is a weekend where 3500+ students can come and experience competition, rigour with lots of fun and joy. This will be the 6th edition of CSK where we host events in Sports, STEM, Management, Fine Arts & Performance arts, Tamizh & English literary and much more.

CHENNAI STUDENTS KONDATTAM 6.0

"What we see here is a complete transformation in our kids, They absolutely love this space and waits for it every year to happen!"

Aiswarya Krishnan, 2018 Fellow

Chennai Students Kondattam is a space for fellows and students to strenghten their bonds and actually see their students flying beyond their classroom walls.
